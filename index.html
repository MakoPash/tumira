Our Higher Purpose

Making a Difference Through Christ

Pastor Pashapa is dedicated to the spiritual growth of Marondera. By spreading the transformative power of God's word, he seeks to build a community rooted in love, compassion, and unwavering faith.

Through local outreach, mentorship, and biblical teaching, our ministry focuses on bringing the light of Christ to every home, ensuring that no one walks their journey alone.

"His mission is simple: to make the love of Jesus known and felt in every corner of our city."
